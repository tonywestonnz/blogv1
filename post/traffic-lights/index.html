Blindsight

Book Recommendation

Posted by TW on Tuesday, June 4, 2019

I stumbled across the Rifters books by Peters Watts and that lead me to read his 2006 book Blindsight.

This is not a review, just a recommendation. It is a great read. I like Watts’ writing style. Less is more. Leaves you wondering.

Classed as hard science fiction, it’s loosely about first contact and a crew of astronauts sent out to investigate a radio signal.

It is probably 5 years ago when I read the book, and still think about it occasionally.

This spin off project looks interesting, but seems to have stalled - http://www.blindsight.space/
