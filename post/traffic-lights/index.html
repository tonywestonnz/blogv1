Traffic Lights

Are traffic lights dumbing down the population?

Posted by TW on Thursday, July 4, 2019

Traffic Lights lead to incompetence

Traffic lights serve a purpose in certain situations. But like lots of things - have they gone too far? What’s wrong with a round-a-bout?

On the occasion there has been a large scale power outage and the traffic lights are out, it would appear that traffic flows much freer, there are less accidents, and all-round it’s a more pleasent experience. In fact there have been several studies that have shown removing traffic lights improves traffic flow and reduces accidents.

In my opinion traffic lights remove personal responsibility. They dumb down drivers. They remove the requirement for thought and calculation.

It’s the left and right turning filters that do my head in. It seems crazy to me that on one hand we have people flying along, manoeuvring more than 1000kg’s of metal at 100km/hr, yet we can’t trust them to turn at an intersection.

Would drivers not be far more conscientious and careful when they are relying on their own and everyone else’s judgement?

If you remove the requirement for people to think - guess what? They will stop thinking!
