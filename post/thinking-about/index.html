What should I be thinking about?

As an IT professional what is one question you should be regularly asking yourself?

Posted by     "TW" on Wednesday, August 14, 2019

As an IT professional, what is one question you should be regularly asking yourself?

“What changes can I do that means I am not required?”

If you can remove yourself, you have made a change for the better.

You always want to being thinking about Elimination, Simplification, Automation, and Documentation. And that’s the beauty of IT, all these things are possible and often easy.

You want to free up your time to think about the next big thing, rather than spending your time completing tasks.
